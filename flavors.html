Ice Creams

Mint Chip

DoTerra's Moroccan mint essence is the refreshing element that contrasts the chunks of dark chocolate.

Salted Caramel

A slightly vanilla cream ontop of a layer of mocha fudge, drizzled with handmade caramel, and topped with a pink himalayan salt.

Strawberry Balsamic

Farm fresh strawberries are ground into a base and added to our vanilla bean base. Top it off with some thick balsamic vinegar glaze and what more could you want?

Super Chocolate

Chocolate to the 3rd power. Using Parliament chocolate for the cream base, the fudge layer on the bottom, and the chocolate chunks, it will make you cookoo for cocoa.

Birthday Cake Batter

The flavor when in doubt. This addicting decadent cream goes with almost anything, and is great for any occasion. It is a must-try for anyone who hasn't tried it. Spread the word.

Maple and Bacon

This maple and molasses based cream is quintessential comfort food. Served with bits of bacon, nubs of pretzels, and optional chocolate chunks. Salty and sweet.

Sorbets

Lemon

A very refreshing treat for a hot day. Suggested ground garnishes are fresh basil, mint, or both.

Pomegranate

A truly powerfruit delicacy. Less sweet, more crunchy, way less work.

White grape and Rose

An amazing mixture of fresh roses and subtle white grape. This delicate sorbet is a wink to our wine-lovers. ☺

Cantaloupe and Honeydew

A mixture of cantaloupe and honeydew juices make this sorbet naturally sweet, syrupy, and exquisite.
